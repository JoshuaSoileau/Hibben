A Front Porch Community
Locations & Map
Hibben currently includes nearly 150 custom-built homes. Plans are for a total of about 300 houses, along with parks, a pool and a clubhouse. Development of the fifth phase is underway. While all Hibben residents have access to the water through the marshfront trail system and the community docks, the fifth phase will include waterfront homesites – on tree-shaded bluffs above Rat Hall Creek.

Community Resources
From Hibben, pools, playgrounds and even the local elementary school are within a mile. The Belle Hall Shopping Center is within five minutes… Starbucks anyone? The beaches and funky restaurants and bars on Isle of Palms and Sullivan’s Island are within 5-10 miles. And the Charleston International Airport is just 15 miles away, right up I-526.
