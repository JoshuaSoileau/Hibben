Hibben

A Front Porch Community

Locations & Map

Hibben currently includes nearly 150 custom-built homes. Plans are for a total of about 300 houses, along with parks, a pool and a clubhouse. Development of the fifth phase is underway.

While all Hibben residents have access to the water through the marshfront trail system and the community docks, the fifth phase will include waterfront homesites – on tree-shaded bluffs above Rat Hall Creek.

Explore the Phases as you rollover the map to the left and notice all of the interesting things along the way.

Hibben Plantation

Hibben is located just inside Belle Hall Plantation on Long Point Road, Mount Pleasant.

Driving Directions
To get to Hibben, turn west from Long Point Road onto the Belle Hall Parkway – the entrance is just across from the cafes, shopping and offices at Belle Hall Shopping Center. Belle Hall Elementary School is just a mile away, and I-526 is even closer, offering easy access to beaches at Isle of Palms and Sullivan’s Island. Click here for directions using Google Maps.
